漸化式の係数 \(\mathrm{coefficients} = \{ c_0, c_1, ..., c_{k-1} \}\)と初期値 \(\mathrm{init} = \{a_0,a_1,...,a_{k-1}\}\)から \(k\)階線形漸化式 \(a_n = a_{n-1} \cdot c_{k-1} + a_{n-2} \cdot c_{k-1} + \dots +a_{n-k} \cdot c_0\)の第 \(n\)項を求めます.

制約

  • \(\mathrm{add}(x, \mathrm{zero}()) = \mathrm{add}(\mathrm{zero}(), x) = x\)
  • \(\mathrm{mul}(x, \mathrm{one}()) = \mathrm{mul}(\mathrm{one}(), x) = x\)
  • \(n \ge 0\)
